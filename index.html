WELCOME TO THE DUNGEON!


Type 'start' to begin your adventure.

Instructions

You're stuck in the maze and your objective is to find the exit. On the road you will have to defeat creatures and collect treasures that increases your score.

Every command you do have to be done through text input.

Movement

You can change rooms typing directions 'north', 'south', 'east' and 'west'. You can only change rooms when all room encounters has been cleared.

Combat

When you encounter a creature you will have to fight it. You can attack by typing command that will be announced on the screen.

Every enemy deducts 100 points from your score every 2 seconds. Better beat them fast!

Flags

To help you navigate through the dungeon you can put a flag in the room when it's empty. Type 'flag' followed by typing it's color, you can pick 'red', 'green' or 'yellow'.

Leaving a flag will cost you 100 score points! But will help you not get lost.

Back to menu

At any point in the dungeon you can type 'start' again to be asked if you want to return to main menu
